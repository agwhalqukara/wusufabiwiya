Taylor Swift Meets Godson, Jaime King's Baby Leo: Adorable Photos!

August 24, 2024
blog

Too cute! Taylor Swift took to Instagram on Tuesday, July 28, to share some adorable photos of her first meeting with Leo Thames, the newborn son of her BFF Jaime King and King's husband, Kyle Newman. 

The "Bad Blood" singer, 25, is the godmother to the tiny baby, who was born on July 16. Little Leo is the second son for King, 36, and Newman, 39, who welcomed their first child James in October 2013.

Swift first shared a sweet photo of herself holding her godson in her arms. Leo already sports a full head of light brunette locks and gives a slight smile in the photo with his eyes closed.

The Grammy winner, who gazes down at the baby with King by her side, captioned the image, "Meeting my boy. @jaime_king @kyle_newman."

"Your little hand's wrapped around my finger and it's so quiet in the world tonight…" she wrote, referencing the lyrics to her song "Never Grow Up" off of the 2010 album Speak Now.
