The Walters Share Indie Pop Gem &quot;Stuck In My Ways&quot;

July 11, 2024
blog

The Walters are back with a highly anticipated new single and music video entitled “Stuck In My Ways”. The song is the first of the year from this viral hit-making group.

The Walters are known from their enthralling fusion of surf rock, alt pop and 70’s yacht rock inspired sounds. The band has an uncanny ability to create mesmerizing harmony-driven releases that are breezy and carefree. “Stuck In My Ways” is no exception with infectious melodies, Beach Boys inspired vocals and glowing pop rock landscapes. The song is about routine and repetition in habits. The band confides the song, “was inspired by repeating the same actions in your day to day life that get you in trouble with your partner.” The music video is a silly and fun watch complete with the band competing in various field day games including potato sack race, corn hole competition, and hot dog eating contest.

The Walters’ music has resonated with listeners all around the globe. With their authentic lyrics and timeless songwriting, the band has generated over 10 billion TIKTOK views and their hit song “I Love You So” can be heard on Top 40 radio all around the world.
