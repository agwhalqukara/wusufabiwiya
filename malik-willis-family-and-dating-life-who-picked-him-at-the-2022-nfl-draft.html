Malik Willis is an American football quarterback who plays for the Tennessee Titans in NFL. Previously, he played college football at Auburn University and Liberty University.

Malik Willis Wiki and Family; Age, Parents, College, Height

The player was born Malik Antonio Willis on 25 May 1999 in College Park, Georgia, the United States. Malik is now 23 years of age. He holds American nationality and African-American ethnicity.

Malik was born the eldest of three kids to his parents, Harold Willis and Shastca Grier. He has two younger siblings: a brother named Marquis Willis and a sister named Destini Willis. Marquis plays football like his elder brother and Destini is a cheerleader.

Malik Willis with his siblings (Source: Instagram)

Malik enrolled at Westlake High School in Atlanta and later transferred to Roswell High School in Roswell, Georgia. Eventually, he played college football at Auburn University and later at Liberty University.

The quarterback has a tall height of 6 feet and 1 inch (1.85 meters). Similarly, Malik weighs around 219 pounds (99 kg).

Malik Willis NFL Draft, Contract, and Salary

Malik was picked by the Tennessee Titans as the 86th overall pick in the third round of the 2022 NFL Draft. It was after he had a tremendous college career at both Auburn and Liberty.

In his 35 college appearances, Malik caught 388 passes for 5,176 yards with 48 touchdowns. Likewise, he rushed for 2,131 yards with 29 touchdowns.

Malik was drafted by the Tennessee Titans in the 2022 NFL Draft (Source: Instagram)

Malik immediately penned a four-year rookie contract with the Titans on 23 July 2022. His contract is worth $5,160,100 and includes a $932,800 signing bonus, $932,800 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $1,290,025. Malik’s salary for 2022 is $705,000.

The 23-year-old made his NFL debut in Week 2 against the Buffalo Bills. So far, he has caught 7 passes for 61 yards in three games.
