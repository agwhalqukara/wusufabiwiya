Holly Madison Celebrates Daughter Rainbow's Second Birthday

August 27, 2024
blog

 

Rainbow’s turning 2 — and it’s time for a very special kind of pizza party!

Holly Madison and husband Pasquale Rotella celebrated their daughter Rainbow Aurora’s birthday on Thursday, March 5.

 

The former Girls Next Door star posted multiple Instagram photos on Thursday, one being a screencap from her 2013 E! TV special, Holly Has a Baby. The photo, captioned, “Two years ago!!!!” shows Madison and Rotella fawning over baby Rainbow after she was just born.

The beautiful blue-eyed toddler got a special cake in the shape of her favorite character, the famous meme, PizzaCat. The three-tiered birthday cake was made to look like a pizza, with PizzaCat sitting at the top.
