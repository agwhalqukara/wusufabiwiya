Snickers Super Bowl XLIX Ad 2015: Danny Trejo Is Brady Bunch's Marcia

August 10, 2024
blog

Here's the story, of a man named Danny! A follow-up of Snickers' hilarious new 2015 Super Bowl ad, featuring Danny Trejo as Marcia Brady, was released on Thursday, Jan. 29, after the first commercial was teased last week. 

The fresh new ads from the chocolate company are part of Snickers' beloved "You're Not You When You're Hungry" campaign, and attempts to illustrate how even America's most picture-perfect family can behave when they're craving a Snickers.

The first teaser, which presumably took place inside the beloved Brady Bunch home, began with a wig-wearing Trejo staring into a mirror and channeling his best Marcia Brady impression.

"103… 104…" says the bulky and intimidating movie villain, 70, as he brushes his hair with extreme intensity. (Trejo is known for starring in films like Machete, Machete Kills, Bad Ass, Desperado, and more.) While the gruff star styles his hair, a knock is heard at the door and Carol Brady (Florence Henderson) sings: "Marcia!"

The Sons of Anarchy actor's reaction, unfortunately, is neither flowery nor Marcia Brady-like.

The second full-length trailer involves Carol and Mike Brady, and again, an angry Trejo as Marcia.
