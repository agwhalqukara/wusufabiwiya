Red Carpet Prankster Vitalii Sediuk Targets Miranda Kerr, Anna Wintour

August 11, 2024
blog

He strikes again! Red carpet prankster Vitalii Sediuk — known for crawling beneath America Ferrera‘s gown at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival and shoving Kim Kardashian while she and Kanye West exited a limo at the Balmain show during last year’s Paris Fashion Week — targeted more A-listers in France this year.

But stars are getting more savvy at shrugging off the infamous rascal and Sediuk’s tricks are getting less and less effective. While Miranda Kerr attended the Louis Vuitton runway show in Paris on Oct. 7, the boldly dressed troublemaker, 26, snuck up and kissed her on the cheek.

The mom to Flynn Bloom, 4, remained poised and walked away from the scene Sediuk caused outside the show. Because she was able to defuse the situation, what really stood out was the model’s perfect fall outfit. She wore a floral shirt with a black leather skirt and a forest green leather jacket (all by Louis Vuitton, naturally), and made the outfit fresh with statement-making combat boots.
